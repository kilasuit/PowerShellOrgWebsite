Join us on Wednesday, May 6th for our second Toronto PowerShell User’s Group meeting.  This time you get to take the wheel!  Send us some of your PowerShell related challenges and we’ll pick the top ones to work out in a group together!  We’ll also be talking about some of the things learned at PowerShell Summit – North America, and more!  
<!--more-->


[Hit us up and let us know ](http://powershellto.ca/contact/)what PowerShell challenges you’d like to table at the next PowerShellTO meeting!  
For this meeting, we'll be located at the Microsoft Technology Center in Mississauga at 1950 Meadowvale Blvd - Mississauga, Ontario, L5N8L9.  Space is limited, so be sure to claim your EventBrite ticket below!  
A note on parking: When you arrive at the MTC, go around to the side facing Meadowvale Blvd.  There is a visitor entrance and parking there.  See you soon!
