I'm collecting a list of known problems in DSC v5 _that have been solved. _Like the infamous "MI RESULT 12" error that could happen if you upgraded from prerelease v5 to production preview. I'm going to document these in "The DSC Book," including in its free sample version, to help preserve these things in one place.  
Again - these need to be _solved_ problems. Just drop as much description as you can into a comment here, and feel free to link to the fix, or to a discussion thread on the problem.  
And please - pass this around. If you've never had a chance to contribute to "the community" before, now's a great time. Even if it's a problem that you know doesn't exist in the _current_ v5 release, let's please just document its former existence.  
Thanks!
