We've posted the first draft of the Program Guide, including the Agenda, for PowerShell + DevOps Global Summit 2017. You'll find it linked near the top of the [Registration Page][1]. If you're attending Summit, please check back a few days before the event to download the final version. We'll have some hardcopies on site, but you'll want to have the PDF downloaded to your pocket computer for easy reference.  
The Guide includes a bunch of tips and information beyond the Agenda, so we heartily recommend that everyone take the time to peruse its 12 pages of goodness.
