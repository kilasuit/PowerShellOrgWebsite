The Denver PowerShell User Group will be meeting again on September 4th and we will have [Keith Hill][1] presenting. Keith has published an ebook, is a repeat Microsoft MVP, and has been heavily involved in writing and maintaining the PowerShell Community Extensions.  
You can find more information on the event as well as RSVP here:
