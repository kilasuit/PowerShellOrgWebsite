You're a quarter of the way to Iron Scripter. In this challenge - [Iron Scripter Prequel Puzzle 4][1] - 
you'll be asked to find a way to make legacy command line tools work with the PowerShell pipeline
.

Please remember that we're not grading submissions for these puzzles. You can comment, and discuss the puzzle on the Iron Scripter Prequel forum.  
If anyone is really stuck or doesn't understand something in the puzzle leave a comment here and I'll try to answer. No guarantees on timeframe though.  
Good luck
