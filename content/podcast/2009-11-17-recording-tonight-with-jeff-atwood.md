[![](http://www.codinghorror.com/blog/images/coding-horror-official-logo-small.png)][1] We are very excited to welcome [Jeff Atwood][1], perhaps better known as [@codinghorror][2] on Twitter, to the podcast tonight. 

Jeff has an extremely popular blog at [CodingHorror.com][1] where he discusses software development best practices. In addition, he is one of the masterminds behind [StackOverflow][3] and [ServerFault][4], two very important websites for PowerShell hackers.

It should be a great interview, Jeff is a very dynamic speaker as you already know if you listen to the [StackOverflow podcast][5].
