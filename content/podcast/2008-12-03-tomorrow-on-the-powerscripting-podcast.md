Our special guest will be [Doug Finke][1].

Doug has been doing software development for more than 25 years. He started on the Mainframe as a Basic Assembly Language developer. The first scripting languages he encountered were REXX and CLIST. He saw how productive these approaches were and sought them out in each new environment he worked with. He has played with Lua, Tcl/tk, Ruby, Python, Groovy and PowerShell since it was called Monad. Doug currently works at [Lab49](http://Lab49.com) a technology consulting firm that  
builds advanced solutions for the financial services industry in NY and London.
