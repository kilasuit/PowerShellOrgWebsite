![](http://powerscripting.files.wordpress.com/2013/04/markrussinovich-175x208.jpg)This Thursday, April 18, we&rsquo;re 
very, very
 excited to be having [Mark Russinovich][1] on the show! (What? I know, right?) For those of you who don't know who Mark is: you should be ashamed of yourselves! It's Mark Russinovich! 

  * He wrote filemon and regmon and the rest of the NTinternals aka Sysinternals utilities!
  * He wrote a ton of books and articles about Windows internals
  * He was the guy that [found the Sony rootkit][2]
  * He's a two-time novelist of some seriously geeky sci-fi you'd love: [Zero Day][3], and [Trojan Horse][4]
  * He has his own [Wikipedia article][5].

Of course, he's been up to a lot of other cool stuff lately for Microsoft, and we'll get into that on the show, as well as see what he has to say about PowerShell.  


As always, join us live at 9:30 PM EST Thursdays at [http://live.powerscripting.net][6]!
