We are extremely happy to welcome Matt from Intel to talk about their new [Intel Core vPro Processor module][1]. Click through to the blog post to read up on the gory details, but the short version is this: the vPro chipset has an embedded web-services server that shares your onboard NIC and allows you to manage the hardware much like you can with the baseboard management controllers (BMC) in server-class systems. That"™s old news. The new news is that Intel released a PowerShell module that helps you to automate your vPro chips, and Matt will tell us all about it!
