![](http://powerscripting.files.wordpress.com/2013/01/netapp_logo.png) This Thursday we're having Glenn Sizremore ([@glnsize][1]) back on the show! We've had Glenn on the show before to talk about their [Data ONTAP PowerShell Toolkit][2], but this time, we're going to take a step back and talk more generally about NetApp storage systems and what the company has been doing that's interesting in Glenn's area of expertise, which is Microsoft solutions.
