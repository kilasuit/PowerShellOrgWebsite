![](http://powerscripting.files.wordpress.com/2012/08/default.gif)Join us tonight (sorry for the late notice!) as we talk to [Vaibhav Bhandari][1]. He'll be telling us about Microsoft's [HealthVault][2] cloud-based health management product, and how you can automate it using [HvPosh][3], his PowerShell module. Be sure to check out [Vaibhav's intro blog post][4], this is what we'll be talking about!  
Also, he is author of the O'Reilly book - [Enabling Programmable Self with HealthVault][5]. The companion website to the book is [EnablingProgrammableSelf.com][6].
