Be sure to join us live tomorrow night (9/27) at 9:30 PM EDT as we chat with [June Blender][1]![](http://powerscripting.files.wordpress.com/2012/09/xrhoge6u8x9rvg4ao5h3.jpeg) from Microsoft! June has been with Microsoft on the PowerShell team for many years, working as a Senior Programming Writer. We have a lot to talk about with all of the changes to the PowerShell help subsystem with version 3, and the greatly increased number of cmdlets.  
But we can't do it alone! We need for YOU to come to the chatroom and bring your questions for June, and to hang out with your fellow PowerShellers. You can find us at http://live.powerscripting.net (and #PowerScriptingPodcast on Freenode).
