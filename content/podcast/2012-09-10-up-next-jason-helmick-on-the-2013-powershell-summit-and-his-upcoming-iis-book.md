![](http://powerscripting.files.wordpress.com/2012/09/helmick_cover150.jpeg)This Thursday we'll be speaking to [Jason Helmick][1] from [Interface Technical Training][2] about his upcoming book from Manning Press which is titled, [Learn Windows IIS in a Month of Lunches][3]. The book won't ship until the Spring of 2013, but you can get early access from Manning today.  
Also on the agenda, Jason is going to announce plans for the [2013 PowerShell Summit][4] which is the community-organized succession to the very popular PowerShell Deep Dive summits from the past two years.  
Be sure to join us live every Thursday at 9:30 PM EDT at [live.powerscripting.net][5]!
