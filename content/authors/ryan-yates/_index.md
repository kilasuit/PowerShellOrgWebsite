Ryan is a long time PowerShell community member based in the UK. He has helped organise in-person events across the UK as well as internationally. 


As a former 7 time MVP Awardee for PowerShell when it was in the Cloud & Datacenter Management category, Ryan's Tagline there was `Ryan is a strong believer that technology, when used correctly, can improve our daily lives. He enjoys sharing his knowledge because the community, and the people within it, is the best thing about working in IT.` A statement that still rings true today.

Ryan currently is a PowerShell Working Group member in the Cmdlets & Modules, Engine & Interactive-UX groups, helping improve the PowerShell experience for all in future.

Ryan is also a Microsoft Global Community Initative Regional Leader for the UK.
