## Get in Touch

We'd love to hear from you! Whether you have questions, feedback, or want to get involved, here's how to reach us.

### Community Channels

The best way to connect with us and the broader PowerShell community:

- **Discord**: Join our [PowerShell Discord server](https://aka.ms/psdiscord) for real-time chat and support
- **Forums**: Visit [forums.powershell.org](https://forums.powershell.org) for in-depth discussions and Q&A
- **Twitter/X**: Follow us [@powershell_org](https://twitter.com/powershell_org)
- **Bluesky**: Follow us on [Bluesky](https://bsky.app/profile/powershell.org)
- **YouTube**: Subscribe to [PowerShell.org on YouTube](https://youtube.com/PowerShellOrg)

### Contributing

Interested in contributing articles, tutorials, or other content?

- Submit articles and content via our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/PowerShellOrg/PowerShellOrgWebsite)
- Open an issue for content suggestions or corrections

### Summit and Events

For questions about the PowerShell + DevOps Global Summit:

- Visit [powershellsummit.org](https://powershellsummit.org) for event details
- Registration inquiries and sponsorship opportunities are handled through the summit website

### Podcast

Have a guest suggestion or topic idea for The PowerShell Podcast?

- Reach out to us on Discord or Twitter with your ideas

### Report an Issue

Found a bug on the website or have a technical concern?

- Open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/PowerShellOrg/PowerShellOrgWebsite/issues)
