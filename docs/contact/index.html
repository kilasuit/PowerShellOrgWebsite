Get in Touch

We’d love to hear from you! Whether you have questions, feedback, or want to get involved, here’s how to reach us.

Community Channels

The best way to connect with us and the broader PowerShell community:

  • Discord: Join our PowerShell Discord server for real-time chat and support
  • Forums: Visit forums.powershell.org for in-depth discussions and Q&A
  • Twitter/X: Follow us @powershell_org
  • Bluesky: Follow us on Bluesky
  • YouTube: Subscribe to PowerShell.org on YouTube

Contributing

Interested in contributing articles, tutorials, or other content?

  • Submit articles and content via our GitHub repository
  • Open an issue for content suggestions or corrections

Summit and Events

For questions about the PowerShell + DevOps Global Summit:

  • Visit powershellsummit.org for event details
  • Registration inquiries and sponsorship opportunities are handled through the summit website

Podcast

Have a guest suggestion or topic idea for The PowerShell Podcast?

  • Reach out to us on Discord or Twitter with your ideas

Report an Issue

Found a bug on the website or have a technical concern?

  • Open an issue on our GitHub repository
